Introduction $SHEEPPD(n)$ is a user made computable function, made by me. It's based off of graphs and is the second function in the animal series. It's made off of graph theory and is equal to the maximum amount of shapes you can make with $n^n + 2^{n^{10^{27}}}$ lines and $n^n + 2^{n^{10^{27}}}$ different colors. The rules There are some rules for $SHEEPPD(n)$: $$\begin{array}{ll} 1.\text{ }n \text{ must be a positive integer. } \\ 2. \text{ Every shape has to be a polygon but doesn't have to be convex. } \\ 3. \text{ A shape can contain itself, as long as it has at least one different color. This only applies if it hasn't been in that shape 5 times, and for example, putting Shape A inside Shape B, both with a different color, then shape B into shape A, both with a different color, is not allowed.} \\ 4. \text{ Lines obviously can't overlap or be directly inside each other. } \\ 5. \text{ Every line has to be a straight line and can't be curved.} \end{array}$$
